SUNRISE BACKCOUNTRY CABIN

Beneath towering Spruce trees, 2.5 miles deep along the Cheat Mountain Ridge Trail, you'll discover Snowshoe Mountain's hidden gem, the Sunrise Backcountry Hut. Spectacular views of the Greenbrier River Valley below greet each guest upon arriving at the Hut.

Minimum: 12 guests. Maximum: 20 guests.

MOUNTAIN LODGE

One of Snowshoe Mountain's most popular outdoor ceremony locations, the Mountain Lodge Lawn is expansive and offers beautiful views. The Ballroom is just steps away, making this a most convenient location for your wedding party! Mountain Lodge also offers a variety of options for smaller, more intimate parties.

Minimum: 25 guests. Maximum: 225 guests.
